OrbiSurge: Galaxy Sling — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

OrbiSurge: Galaxy Sling ("the Game") is a mobile game for iOS published by Mustafa Cerit ("we"). This policy explains what data the Game handles and how.

Data we collect

The Game does not require an account and does not collect, store or transmit personal information to our own servers. Your progress, scores, coins, owned rockets and settings are stored locally on your device, and — if iCloud is enabled on your device — mirrored to your private iCloud key-value storage so progress follows you across your own devices. We cannot read your iCloud data.

Third-party services

  • Apple Game Center — if you are signed in to Game Center, your scores are submitted to Apple's leaderboards and your achievements to Apple's achievement service under your Game Center profile. This is handled by Apple according to the Apple Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — the Game may show advertisements served by Google AdMob after a failed run. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads, as described in the Google Privacy Policy. On iOS, ad tracking only occurs if you allow it via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. Purchasing "Remove Ads" disables all advertising.
  • Apple In-App Purchase — purchases (Remove Ads and coin packs) are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Notifications

If you grant permission, the Game schedules local daily-mission reminders on your device. These are local notifications only — nothing is sent to a server, and you can revoke the permission in iOS Settings at any time.

Network use

The Game performs a lightweight connectivity check at launch and uses the network only for the third-party services listed above.

Children

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are configured for a general audience through AdMob's settings.

Your choices

  • Deny ad tracking via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • Purchase Remove Ads to disable advertising entirely.
  • Sign out of Game Center in iOS Settings to stop leaderboard and achievement submissions.
  • Disable iCloud Drive for the Game in iOS Settings to stop cloud progress sync.
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data.

Changes

We may update this policy; changes will be published on this page.
